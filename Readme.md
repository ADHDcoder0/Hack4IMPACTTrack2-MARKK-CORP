📌 Approved Problem Statement

Inefficiency in Industrial Waste Trading in Urban Ecosystems

Rapid urbanization and industrial growth in smart cities generate massive amounts of reusable waste. However:

Industries lack efficient systems to channel waste into reuse pipelines

Recyclers face inconsistent and fragmented supply

Waste trading is dominated by middlemen, increasing costs

Lack of transparency leads to unfair pricing and resource loss

This results in poor resource utilization, increased landfill burden, and inefficiencies in sustainable urban infrastructure.

💡 Solution Overview

ScrapSetu is an AI-powered B2B waste marketplace designed for smart cities that:

Digitizes industrial waste flows

Enables direct supplier–recycler connections

Uses AI for smart matching and pricing

Provides buyer recommendations and waste assessment

Integrates logistics for end-to-end waste movement

By transforming waste into a structured resource stream, ScrapSetu supports circular economy models and strengthens sustainable urban infrastructure.
